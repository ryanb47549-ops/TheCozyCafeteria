Our Story

A home away
from home

Tucked along Onehunga Mall, The Cozy Cafeteria is a warmly-lit spot that feels like Nana's kitchen — mismatched furniture, cosy corners, and the smell of freshly brewed Allpress coffee in the air.

The team welcomes everyone: early morning regulars, families, solo travellers catching the next train, and groups settling in for a long brunch. The food is honest, generous, and made with care.

Whether you're here for the famous Big Breakfast, a slice of cheesecake, or just a strong flat white — there's always a warm seat waiting for you.
